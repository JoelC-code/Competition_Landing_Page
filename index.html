About

The "Bank Kotak Business Competition" is a national event promoting innovation and entrepreneurship. Our website provides all necessary information, including competition details, schedule, rules, and registration. Participants can benefit from networking opportunities, exciting prizes, and skill development. Join us to turn your business ideas into reality and make a lasting impact.

Rules

In the competition, the following has been enforced:

  1. Plagiarism is forbidden and can be resulted in an instant disqualification
  2. Participant must come 30 minutes before the competition start
  3. Jury's scoring is absolute and cannot be changed after the competition
  4. Late tolerance is 15 minutes after the competition started, if not appearing then disqualification for desertion
  5. It's reccomended for all team members to come to the competition

For more information, you can check the guidebook thats given.
